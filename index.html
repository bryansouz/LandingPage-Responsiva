Our roots


JSBJJ is a well-established team that has been growing visibly since 2011; in those years the club has opened affiliations in Dublin and around Europe. In our club we teach values and characters along with top level jiu jitsu; our goal is to create a winning person on and off the mat.

Our coaches are driven by a passion for martial arts, in and out the dojo. One of the most positive environments you will ever be in, the club is filled with friendly people that constantly try to improve themselves and work hard to reach their goals.
